# Project Vision

## Vision

To become a leading intelligent platform for Internal Audit, Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance (GRC), empowering organizations to improve governance, strengthen internal controls, and support better decision-making through technology and artificial intelligence.

---

## Mission

To develop a professional, secure, and scalable Internal Audit Platform that helps organizations plan, execute, monitor, and improve audit activities in accordance with the Global Internal Audit Standards (GIAS), COSO Framework, and international best practices.

---

## Objectives

The platform aims to:

- Improve Internal Audit efficiency.
- Enhance audit quality.
- Strengthen governance and internal control.
- Support risk-based auditing.
- Simplify audit reporting.
- Track recommendations and corrective actions.
- Build a centralized audit knowledge base.
- Integrate Artificial Intelligence into audit activities.

---

## Target Users

- Internal Audit Departments
- Chief Audit Executives (CAE)

- Executive Management
