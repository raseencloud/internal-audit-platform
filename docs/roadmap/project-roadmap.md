# Project Roadmap

## Vision

Develop a world-class Internal Audit Platform that enables organizations to manage Internal Audit activities according to the Global Internal Audit Standards (GIAS), COSO Framework, Governance, Risk Management, and Compliance (GRC) best practices.

---

## Mission

Build a professional, secure, scalable, and AI-powered Internal Audit solution that improves audit quality, enhances governance, and supports value creation.

---

# Development Phases

- Initial architecture

- Dashboard

- Release v1.0.0

Status: Completed

## Long-Term Vision

Become one of the leading Internal Audit platforms supporting governance, risk management, compliance, and AI-assisted auditing.
